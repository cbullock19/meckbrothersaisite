About Meck Brothers

Building trust, quality, and lasting relationships in New Jersey for over 24 years.

Our Story

Founded in 2000 by brothers Michael and David Meck, Meck Brothers Construction began as a small family operation with a big vision: to transform the way people think about construction and remodeling in New Jersey.

What started as a two-man crew has grown into one of the most trusted construction companies in the region, but we've never lost sight of our roots. We're still a family business at heart, treating every client like they're part of our extended family.

Over the past 24+ years, we've completed hundreds of projects across New Jersey, from kitchen remodels to custom home builds. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship, transparent communication, and honest pricing has earned us the trust of countless families throughout the state.

Our Vision

"Total focus on the families we build for" - setting the quality standard while keeping the process clear and stress-free.

Our Mission

To make the remodeling process smoother, more personal, and built around the real lives of New Jersey families.

Our Process

Planning

We start by understanding your vision, budget, and timeline. Our detailed planning phase ensures every aspect of your project is carefully considered.

Design

Our design phase brings your vision to life with detailed plans, material selections, and 3D renderings to help you visualize the final result.

Build

With experienced craftsmen and quality materials, we bring your project to life with precision, care, and attention to every detail.

Deliver

We don't just complete projects - we deliver exceptional results that exceed expectations and stand the test of time.
